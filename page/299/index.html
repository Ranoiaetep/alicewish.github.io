Aside from being presented in a large format that really displays Jaime Hernandez’s stunning art, God and Science will be a “director’s cut” version that includes a full 30 new pages in addition to the original 100-page epic, including four new full-color faux Ti-Girls covers, several expansions of scenes, an epilogue set back in Maggie’s apartment, and a long fantasy/timewarp sequence that draws the focus back on Penny’s awful predicament.

“For what’s essentially an evocative throwback to the kid’s superhero comics of yore, there’s a lot going on here–youth versus seasoned oldsters, absolute power corrupting absolutely, mother/child dysfunction–and it’s all wrapped up in a package of terrific dialogue, stellar artwork, and enough raw fun to drown in.” - Publishers Weekly
